## What I Do  
I build data pipelines and visualizations that help journalists, analysts and the public explore political and economic data.

* **Gather & simplify** messy & complex public data (e.g., campaign finance, polling, media coverage, public websites)  
* **Visualize & publish** interactive dashboards, maps, and data graphics to create takeaways from complex data   
* **Collaborate & iterate** with journalists, academics, and the public. Improve products and keep them up to date over time
